BRIEF HISTORY AND DEVELOPMENT OF SEASPIMEX VIET NAM

On 01-09-1983, Business Department of Seaprodex was seperated from the company and formed as Special Aquatic Products Import Export Company – SEASPIMEX VIETNAM. This event has shown a proof of successful development of SEASPRODEX as a whole and SEASPIMEX Vietnam in particular.

Over the past 30 years of development, Seaspimex VIETNAM has contributed significantly to the Vietnam’s economic transformation. Not only has SEASPIMEX focused on trading and creating thousands of jobs but the company has also made tremendous contribution on social activities.
