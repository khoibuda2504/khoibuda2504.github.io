About us

Beginning in early 2009, Come and Go has had a clear vision of providing private upscale travel experiences through Vietnam and S.E. Asia.
We offer set programs through some of the most popular destinations as well as Tailor made itineraries that we create with you, to fit your interests, timeframe and exceed your expectations.
We believe that, whether you’re travelling for culture, adventure, relaxation, shopping or enlightenment, the most important thing is that your experience should be enjoyable – and you can only enjoy yourself when you have nothing to worry about. That’s why we employ friendly, communicative sales & operations staff to help you arrange your tour, and why we are very, very fussy about the hotels, drivers and guides we arrange to look after you when you’re here. We vet all our suppliers very carefully and make them aware of the standards we expect from them.
With our office of 5 dedicated people, you're assured a quality tour program. Other travel companies have 5 levels worth of staff, they treat you like a number, and sadly, you are a number. At Come and Go Vietnam each member of our team is involved in our tailor made programming and constantly inspecting new and exciting tour options for our valued guests.
And we don’t just sell Vietnam – we also have partner offices in Cambodia & Laos for clients who want to do multi-country tours or who have already visited Vietnam & want to try somewhere new.
So have fun exploring our website – why not start by finding out more about our staff below; they’re waiting to help arrange a memorable holiday for you! We hope we can work with you and welcome you to Vietnam soon.
All the best,
