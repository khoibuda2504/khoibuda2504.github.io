Tailor made

You’ll find plenty of original, off-the-shelf tour programmes on our site, but the vast majority of our clients end up tailoring a tour to their own specifications, whether it’s tweaking one of our existing tours or coming up with their own personalised itinerary from scratch.

All our tailor made programmes are developed after lengthy consultation with the customer to find out what their interests are, what style of accommodation they like, how active they are, how old their children are etc. We use this information to create a suitable programme that uses appropriate hotels and guides.
Tailor made doesn’t have to be expensive either – sure, it costs more than travelling with a group on an inflexible itinerary, but you’ll find that the flexibility and spontaneity of a bespoke tour are worth the extra cost. And we are committed to offering value for money, whether you’re travelling by train & staying in 3* hotels, or travelling in a private limo & staying in 5* luxury!
