Luxury travel

Surprising though it may seem to many, Vietnam is establishing a great reputation as a luxury travel destination. A wide range of 5* city hotels and luxurious beach resorts, a growing number of fine dining restaurants, and a selection of our own exclusive “insider” experiences make it easy to enjoy the more luxurious side of this emerging travel destination.
At Come & Go, we understand that luxury travel isn’t about ostentation or flamboyance, or spending as much money as is humanly possible. It’s about privacy, discretion, comfort, intimacy and service. It’s about enjoying unique experiences. And, like any other travel demographic, it’s about value for money.
Our luxury travellers may be staying in deluxe 5* accommodation, but they can equally be found enjoying an authentic pho breakfast at a street restaurant, riding a pushbike down a jungle trail in the Mekong Delta, or enjoying morning exercise with the locals around Hoan Kiem lake in Hanoi. For our clients, access to experiences that most travellers simply don’t get is key.
From VIP fast-track meet & greet at the airport to a luxury limousine airport drop-off & farewell gift, we cover all points in between, including competitive rates at some of Southeast Asia’s most exclusive & luxurious properties; guides who are absolute experts in their specialist fields; private boats, junks and helicopters; private cooking classes with celebrity chefs; private ceremonies at Buddhist or Cao Dai temples; or simply spending time with interesting local people. If you want to do it, we can arrange it.
To arrange your own tour, please email us at inquiries@comeandgovietnam.com.
